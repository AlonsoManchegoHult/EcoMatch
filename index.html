The Crisis We Can't Ignore

Fast fashion's affordability and speed come at severe environmental and social costs. Gen Z and Millennials use fashion to express identity, yet this unintentionally fuels overconsumption.

Over 100 billion garments are produced annually, contributing to 10% of global carbon emissions, labor exploitation, and overproduction. Social media FOMO drives constant trend cycles. Sustainable options often feel too expensive or inaccessible.

The core challenge: redesign consumption so sustainable choices feel as exciting, validating, and expressive as fast fashion.

What We Discovered

Consumers are willing to pay 9-12% more for sustainable goods, yet price remains the top barrier. Demand exists for affordable, truly eco-friendly products with transparent sustainability proof.

Our Net-Positive Innovation

A dating-app-inspired platform that matches users with sustainable brands based on their style and values. Users build a style profile (aesthetic, brands they love, causes they care about). EcoMatch connects them to verified sustainable companies aligned with their identity.

  • Solves "I never know what to wear"
  • Eliminates greenwashing confusion
  • Makes discovery fun & effortless
